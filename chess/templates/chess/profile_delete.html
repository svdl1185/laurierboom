You are about to initiate the process of deleting your Bar Blitz profile. Please read the following information carefully before proceeding.

What happens when you delete your profile:

  • Your personal information (name, email, chess account IDs) will be removed
  • Your account will be deactivated and you will no longer be able to log in
  • Your tournament history will be preserved but anonymized
  • Your ratings and statistics will remain in the system for tournament integrity
  • You will need to create a new account if you wish to participate in future tournaments

If you are currently registered in any active tournaments, you will not be able to delete your profile until those tournaments are completed.

Please note that this action cannot be undone.
